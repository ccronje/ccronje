### Hi there 👋

I'm a librarian with an engineering background and ten years of [experience](https://www.linkedin.com/in/carmi-cronje-68a590104/) in research data management and [open science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_science). I enjoy working with highly collaborative people who have a focus on knowledge sharing and are willing to pause and ask questions like "why are we doing this?". I've studied fine arts at the [NAS](https://nas.edu.au/) and love to experiment with creative approaches to problem solving.

I'm a LGBTQIA+ ally and care about the well-being of those around me. I practice meditation and organise sessions for staff. Over the last year I've been assisting [Bhante Sujato](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhante_Sujato) and a community of Pāli scholars with the publication of computer assisted translations of the Early Buddhist Texts to [SuttaCentral](https://suttacentral.net/).
